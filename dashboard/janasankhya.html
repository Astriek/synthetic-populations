Layers 2 + 3 — convergent validity

Two independent imputation methods agree: AI-usability scores from structural priors (Layer 2) and from LLM-persona reasoning (Layer 3) match within ±1 for 100% of workers (correlation 0.062); rights-awareness mean abs error 0.086.

Data: IHDS-II individual file (ICPSR 36151, DS0001). Model: CTGAN (Xu et al., NeurIPS 2019), trained 300 epochs on 45,424 real informal workers. AI audit run against the local claude model.
